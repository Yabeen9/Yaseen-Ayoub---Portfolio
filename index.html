Work

Tesla - Computer Aided Engineering (CAE) Intern

At Tesla, I built a mesh model of the vehicle side door using ANSA, tested dynamic load cases using LS-DYNA, analyzed post-processing results, and presented design changes. This experience honed my skills in CAE and provided me with hands-on experience in the automotive industry.

PepsiCo - Supply Chain and Engineering Intern

During my internship at PepsiCo, I optimized the conveyor system integrated with ANCRA at a manufacturing plant. I developed the Run Right Process and created a real-time Excel data tracking tool for ANCRA and its conveyor system, improving efficiency and productivity.

Research Assistant - Micromechanics

As a Research Assistant, I improved the accuracy of a uniaxial testing apparatus for micro to millimeter scale samples, researched material characterization methods, and enhanced the resolution and repeatability of force measurements.

Teaching Assistant - Structural Mechanics Laboratory & Principles of Mechanical Design at ASU

In my role as a Teaching Assistant, I performed laboratory demonstrations, assisted students in understanding mechanical design concepts, and provided real-world applications of Structural Mechanics.

Projects

Nonlinear FEA of Bolted Nozzle Flange used in Saturn V Rocket

Performed stress analysis using ANSYS, modeled the nozzle flange with contact conditions, and verified FEA results with hand calculations.

Devils Invent Competition

Led a team to develop a prototype that alerted drowsy or distracted drivers using Arduino, Python, and SolidWorks. Secured funding from PayPal after winning second place.

Mechanical Engineering Senior Year Capstone Project - OPTIMIC

Worked as the Test and Analysis Engineer on a team of 6 engineers to create a multi-functional gym bar named OPTIMIC. Performed structural analysis using ANSYS Static Structural, conducted various analyses like FMEA, DFMA, DTC, reliability, and maintainability, developed and executed comprehensive testing procedures, and presented results to industry professionals and faculty.

Topology Optimization of Beam Design

Utilized the Topology Optimization tool paired with Static Structural in ANSYS to perform Von-Mises stress analysis, identified potential structural failure points, reduced the mass of the beam by 20%, and produced a manufacturable design using SpaceClaim-CAD software.

Micromechanics Research
